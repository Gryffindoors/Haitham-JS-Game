Tic-Tac-Toe ()

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe, commonly known as (XO) Game

Rules:
  • Two players can play this game.
  • Each player will have a symbol, one will have 'X' and the other will have 'O'.
  • Player 'X' will play first, then player 'O'.
  • Players will take turns to place their symbol on the board.
  • The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
  • If the board is full and no player has won, the game will be a draw.
